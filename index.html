What is the yield to call calculator?
The yield to call calculator is a tool that determines the return on investment on a callable bond.
How to calculate yield to call?
To calculate yield to call you should use the following formula:
yield to call = (annual interest + ((call price - market price) / number of years until call)) / ((call price + market price) / 2) * 100
Why use yield to call calculator?
The yield to call calculator is used in order to measure the return on investment on a callable bond.
